MORE DETAIL ON CYBERSECURITY

Protecting The Digital World

CyberSecurity is an interdisciplinary academic field that focuses on the protection of digital information networks, and systems from illegal access. For Instance; think of a house as a computer system:
  • The doors and windows represent the entry points to the system (network, software, etc.).
  • The locks and security systems represent the safeguards (firewalls, password, etc.).
  • The valuables inside the house represent the sensitive data (personal info, financila data, etc.).
Just as you would secure your house with locks, alarms, and security cameras, cybersecurity involves protecting your computer systems and data from theft or damages.

Key Features Of Cybersecurity:

  • Confidentiality: Protecting sensitive data from unauthorized access.
  • Integrity: Ensuring data is accurate, complete, and modified without autorization.
  • Availability: Ensuring systems, network, and data are accesible and usable when needed.
  • Authentication: Verifying the identity of users, devices, and systems to ensure legal access
