Machine learning is a subset of artificial intelligence(AI) that involves the development of algorithms and statistical models that enable computers to learn from data, decisions, and improve their performance on a tas over time. For Instance; think of machine learning as a car :
  • Car collects data on driving habits.
  • Algorithms learns drving patterns
  • Algorithms predicts driver's next move
  • Car adjusts settingsI(e.g., temperature, music accordingly)
This example illustrate how machine learning enables a car to learn from data, acts, and adject settings automatically

  • Self-Improvement: Machine Learning models improves over timeas they learn from new data.
